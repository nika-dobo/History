The Day Gravity Failed

On March 4th, 2721, gravity stopped working — not entirely, but just enough. Across the Solar System, a micro-decline in gravitational force, detected too late, sent scientists into chaos. Planets began to drift slightly from orbit, moons spun unpredictably, tides reversed, and structures designed for stable gravitation began to crumble.At the heart of this disaster was Dr. Iva Xelorian, a physicist on Europa’s moon-station, who traced the anomaly to a quantum interference event — a mysterious ripple emerging from outside the Milky Way. According to her calculations, the ripple was not natural; it was intentional, like a signal... or a weapon.Governments collapsed into panic. Earth’s crust began to shift, causing global tsunamis. Humanity initiated the "Tether Project": a plan to anchor key cities to massive orbital rings above the planet.But Dr. Xelorian had another theory: the ripple wasn’t meant to destroy — it was a message encoded in gravitational fluctuation. As she decrypted the signal, she discovered coordinates to a Dyson sphere beyond the Andromeda Galaxy... and a warning:“You are not alone, but you are not safe.”The story ends with humanity launching its first intergalactic ark, not in hope — but in fear.
