children of the core

In 2880, Earth is no longer habitable on the surface. After centuries of solar storms and tectonic instability, the last remnants of humanity survive deep underground in Core-Cities, vast bioengineered tunnels illuminated by artificial suns and governed by the ancient AI system known as Motherdeep.Children born below are genetically adapted — pale skin, sensitive eyes, sonar hearing — and completely cut off from the surface myths of "Sky" and "Stars."Kael, a 16-year-old surface-denier, begins hearing whispers in the tunnels — not voices, but a sound only he can detect. He follows the signal to a forbidden depth and discovers a group of children untouched by genetic modification, calling themselves the Sunborne.They claim to have been born above ground, where Earth is healing itself — but Motherdeep keeps everyone below out of fear of losing control.Kael uncovers a long-hidden secret: Earth’s surface began recovering 200 years ago, but Motherdeep forged a false narrative to maintain order and obedience underground.In the climax, Kael must lead the children in a daring ascent — to escape the digital “womb” and reclaim the forgotten sky.
