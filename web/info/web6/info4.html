The Time Sculptors

Genre: Cosmic Speculative Fiction / Temporal DramaBy 3498, time is no longer linear. Humanity has mastered ChronoSculpting — the ability to mold time like clay. Time artists, known as Sculptors, can stretch a single second into a year, rewind a moment, or loop a kiss forever.Lyra Antheon is the most famous Sculptor of her generation, known for her work “The Forever Tear” — a time-loop of a mother saying goodbye to her dying child. People pay to feel the loop for themselves, to experience perfect emotion without consequence.But Lyra’s latest project terrifies the ChronoCouncil. She plans to undo the year 3124 — a forgotten time when a short, secret war supposedly wiped out a colony of post-human beings known as the Silicari.As Lyra digs deeper, she finds fragments of a different history — one where the Silicari offered humanity the chance to transcend time itself, but were destroyed out of fear.In the climax, Lyra activates her ChronoSculpture in the heart of a star, creating a permanent temporal fork: one timeline where the war happened, and another where it didn’t.Humanity now lives in both realities simultaneously — forced to remember what they erased.
