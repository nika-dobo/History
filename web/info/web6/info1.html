The Sky Below

By 3214, the Earth’s surface is abandoned — but not because of war or collapse. Humanity voluntarily migrated into the atmosphere, building floating mega-cities that hover in the upper stratosphere. The surface is left to regenerate naturally, forming the Terra Preserve — a forbidden zone controlled by AI guardians called the Sentinels.The protagonist, Ari Halven, is a “Skyborn” archaeologist, obsessed with what life was like on the surface. She’s one of the few given clearance to descend below, but only under supervision and for brief missions.One day, while excavating a buried library in the Amazon Basin, Ari discovers a sealed book — handwritten, ancient, and not in any digital archive. It contains stories of a secret group that never left the Earth. They’re known as The Deep-Dwellers — rumored to have evolved into something... not quite human.Driven by curiosity, Ari goes rogue. She escapes her mission and disappears into the ruins of Earth, searching for these mysterious people. What she finds is astonishing: a thriving civilization, in tune with nature, who rejected the floating cities’ sterile perfection.They offer Ari a choice: return to her synthetic sky-life, or join them — and never go back.
