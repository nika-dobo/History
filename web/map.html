Georgia

Georgians invented the first backpack sundial, which pointed north with obsidian, a stone from Svaneti.

USA

a special mirror was used in Massachusetts to detect witches, which only revealed 'spots of conscience' in moonlight.

Russia

the first "ice organ" appeared in Moscow, which was used during prayers and its sound depended on the thickness of the ice.

Japan

samurai in Japan created the first paper armor, which became stronger in the rain and was used to protect the shogun.

Egypt

Egyptian oasis priests created an hourglass that showed time not in minutes, but in the calmness of the Nile River.

England

The first "moon shadow race" was held on the River Thames in London, where ships were raced by their reflected shadows on the water.
